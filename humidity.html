Humidity

Suprisingly, humidity was not sugnificantly effected as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). It was determined there was no correlation as seen by the scatter plot displayed. This could be do to thethe time of year the data was gathered, since locations further from the equator are regularly more humid in the summer months and become more dry in the winter months. Since Data was gathered for one day it does not demonstrate the effect year round, thus there may be a correlation during certain months of the year.
